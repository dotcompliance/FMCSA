Complete USDOT Number Registration Service

Starting a motor carrier operation requires navigating complex FMCSA regulations and paperwork. Our expert team simplifies the USDOT registration process, ensuring accurate filing and fast approval so you can focus on growing your business.

A USDOT number is a unique identifier assigned by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) to companies operating commercial motor vehicles. Whether you're hauling freight across state lines, transporting passengers, or carrying hazardous materials, proper USDOT registration is your first step toward legal compliance and operational authority.

We handle every aspect of the registration process, from determining your specific requirements based on your operation type to submitting your MCS-150 form and securing your USDOT number. Our comprehensive service includes verification of all documentation, follow-up with FMCSA, and confirmation of your active status.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a USDOT number?

With our expedited service, you can receive your USDOT number within 24-48 hours. Standard processing through FMCSA typically takes 5-10 business days. We handle all paperwork and follow up to ensure fastest possible processing.

What information do I need to apply for a USDOT number?

You'll need your business information (legal name, EIN, address), type of operation (interstate or intrastate), vehicle information, cargo details, and mileage estimates. We provide a comprehensive checklist and guide you through each requirement.

Do I need a USDOT number if I only operate intrastate?

USDOT number requirements vary by state for intrastate operations. Many states require a USDOT number for commercial vehicles over 10,001 lbs or those transporting hazardous materials. We can help determine your specific state requirements and handle the registration accordingly.
