FAQ

Do I need a hazmat endorsement for my CDL?

Yes, if you transport hazardous materials requiring placards (typically quantities meeting Table 2 of 49 CFR 172.504), your CDL drivers must have an H endorsement obtained through TSA security screening and knowledge testing.

What is the hazmat registration requirement?

Motor carriers transporting certain quantities and types of hazmat must register with FMCSA and pay biennial fees. We determine if you need registration and handle the complete process.
