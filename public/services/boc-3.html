Complete BOC-3 Process Agent Filing

BOC-3 is a mandatory FMCSA requirement for all interstate motor carriers. This form designates process agents in each state where legal documents can be served on your behalf. Without proper BOC-3 filing, your operating authority can be revoked.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BOC-3 and why is it required?

BOC-3 is a form designating process agents in each state where you operate. It's required by FMCSA for all interstate motor carriers to ensure legal documents can be served if needed.

How long does BOC-3 filing take?

We process BOC-3 filings within 24 hours and file with FMCSA immediately. You'll receive confirmation within 1-2 business days.

Do I need BOC-3 for every state?

Yes, BOC-3 requires process agent designation in all 50 states plus DC. We provide nationwide coverage with a single filing.
