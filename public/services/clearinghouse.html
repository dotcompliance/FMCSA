FMCSA Clearinghouse Compliance Made Simple

The FMCSA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse is a secure online database containing records of violations of drug and alcohol prohibitions for holders of Commercial Driver's Licenses (CDLs). As of January 6, 2020, FMCSA requires employers to query the Clearinghouse for current and prospective employees.

Our comprehensive service handles all aspects of Clearinghouse compliance including employer registration, driver consent management, pre-employment full queries, annual limited queries, and violation reporting when required. We ensure you meet all FMCSA requirements while protecting sensitive employee information.

FAQ

What is the FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse?

The Clearinghouse is a federal database containing records of CDL driver drug and alcohol program violations. Employers must query it before hiring drivers and conduct annual queries of current employees.

How often must I query the Clearinghouse?

Employers must conduct full queries before hiring any CDL driver and limited queries annually for all employed CDL drivers. We can manage both requirements for you.

What is driver consent for Clearinghouse queries?

Drivers must provide electronic consent before employers can conduct queries. We help facilitate the driver consent process and maintain proper documentation.
