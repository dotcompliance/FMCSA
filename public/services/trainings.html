FAQ

What DOT training is required for new drivers?

New drivers need Hours of Service training, hazmat training (if applicable), company safety policies training, and may require Entry-Level Driver Training (ELDT) depending on their license class and endorsements.

How often must drivers complete safety training?

FMCSA requires annual training on Hours of Service rules. Hazmat employees must receive training at least every three years. Additional company-specific or state-mandated training may have different frequencies.
